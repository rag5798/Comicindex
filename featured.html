Marvel

My favorite Marvel character is Ghost Rider. He has a couple different forms he takes like Cosmic Ghost Rider, which I think is the most interesting. This is a variation that is Frank Castle as Ghost Rider with powers from Odin. Frank Castle is brought back to life and tries to alter Thanos's timeline to try and make him a good guy. This gives us some of the different timelines in the Infinity War and ultimatly changes nothing. With the power of a time stone Cosmic Ghost Rider reveals that future Thanos wins and he gives up on raising Thanos.

1st appearance of Cosmic Ghost Rider (Frank Castle)

Price: $40

DC

My favorite DC character is Flash. I was able to find a couple note worthy flash comics while searching with at my local comic shop with my dad. Not only does the flash an interesting character but I also have a lot of awesome finds of the flash. The flash starts as a forensic chemist, Barry accidentally gains superpowers following a lightning strike in his laboratory. Like other heroes who go by The Flash, Barry is a "speedster", with powers that derive mainly from his superhuman speed.

Independent

One of my favorite independent characters is G-Man by Chris Giarrusso. I found a comic of these with my dad and thought it was funny beacuse my last name starts with a G. The main character, Mikey G, wanted to be a superhero. He found a magic cape, which gave him the ability to fly, enhanced his strength and gave him some invulnerability. His older brother, Dave G, took a piece of the cape to make a belt. He called himself Great Man.

1st apperance of G-Man (Mickey G)

Price: $2
